Privacy Policy

Debugging data

We use Apple's opt-in service for crash reporting. Your device, version of iOS and the date of when the crash occurred will be viewable by us. However, no personally identifiable information is kept by us or sent externally. The only things we will see are what steps were taken before the crash occurred. We will not see any of the data you've entered such as the car or charger you selected.

Analytics & usage data

We use the privacy focused analytics service AppTelemetry for keeping track on how people use our app and which devices they use. Your device, version of iOS and usage data such as which app features were used most will be viewable by us. However, no personally identifiable information is kept by us or sent externally. The only things we will see are aggregated statistics on which app features, devices, app version and OS version were used.

Purchase history

When you make a donation in the app (which is awesome!) we use a service called RevenueCat to log the amount, an anonymous identifier and the time and date. This allows us to keep track of all donations on a dashboard. No personally identifiable information is kept by us or sent externally for this purpose.

Personal data

Put simply, no personally identifiable data is ever viewable by us. For debugging purposes – when you contact us – we may ask you to provide this kind of information to troubleshoot an issue. Otherwise, your personal data never leaves your device.

Any questions?

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
